Diet

A well-balanced diet is essential for achieving fitness goals, whether it's weight loss, muscle building, or endurance training. A healthy diet should include lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, healthy fats, and essential vitamins to fuel the body and aid recovery. Hydration is key, and processed foods should be minimized. Eating the right nutrients at the right time supports energy levels, muscle recovery, and overall well-being.
