Weight loss

Weight loss exercises help burn calories, boost metabolism, and improve overall fitness. A combination of cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises can accelerate fat loss and enhance endurance. Regular workouts like jumping jacks, burpees, mountain climbers, and running not only aid in weight loss but also improve heart health and muscle tone. Pairing exercises with a balanced diet and consistency is key to achieving sustainable results.
